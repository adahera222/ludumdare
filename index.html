    About

    I decided to make a game for Ludum Dare too! It's about docking a spacecraft to a refueling station with realistic Box2D physics added for extra QWOP-iness. But what does this have to do with the theme "You only get one", you might ask. Well, the spacecraft is going to the refueling station with a reason, it has one tiny tank of fuel left and needs to make it to the station before floating into nothingness. Oh, and you also have limited electricity. cough time cough limit cough
